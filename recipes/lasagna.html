Gluten Free Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is too hard for a normal person to make, and will take forever. I strongly recommend ordering out.

If you refuse to eat out, your next best option is to lower your standards significantly and buy frozen.

Ingredients

  • Frozen lasagna
  • Diet coke
  • Frozen garlic bread

Steps

  1. Read the instructions on the back of the box
  2. Do what it tells you to do
  3. Get that garlic bread in early
  4. Pour some diet coke over some ice
